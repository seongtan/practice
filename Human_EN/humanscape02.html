Rarenote,

a platform for providing personalized information
on rare diseases

Rarenote is a service in which rare disease patients can
identify medicines under development that are suitable for
their needs and participate in clinical trials, based on the
health data they upload on the platform.

My data

From collecting health data to development of
therapeutics

Patients can systematically collect and manage their health
data*. These data can be applied for patient recruitment for
clinical trials by pharmaceutical companies and research
institutions at home and abroad that support efficient new
drug development.

By applying blockchain technology, all data is safely
controlled so that no data can be used without patients’
consent.

*Health data: Patients’ health data including genetic test results,
medical treatment data, and symptom data.

What Rarenote can
make happen
Shortening the Period of Developing
New Pharmaceuticals

Clinical trials, conducted to test the efficacy and
safety of a new drug, are the most time-consuming
and expensive steps in the process of developing
a new medicine. This is because it is tricky and
difficult to recruit patients who meet the exact
sophisticated requirements of a clinical trial.

Equipped with systematically organized health
data, Rarenote is capable of quickly recruiting test
subjects, thereby dramatically shortening the
period of developing new pharmaceuticals.

Enhanced Awareness of Diseases

Rarenote can attract substantial benefits such as
the development of news drugs from
pharmaceutical companies, related government
support and research funds, and widespread
social consensus, by gathering scattered patient
data in a single space and thus enhancing
awareness of diseases.

All these changes can come sooner
with your participation.
